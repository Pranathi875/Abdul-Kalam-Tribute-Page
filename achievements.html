Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Area Achievements
Space Technology Led the development of India’s first Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV-III) and launched Rohini satellite in 1980.
Missile Development Chief architect of India’s IGMDP — developed Agni, Prithvi, Trishul, Akash, and Nag missiles.
Defence & Nuclear Tests Played a key role in the 1998 Pokhran-II nuclear tests as Chief Scientific Adviser.
Presidency Served as 11th President of India (2002–2007); promoted education and technology.
Awards Received Bharat Ratna, Padma Vibhushan, and Padma Bhushan among many honours.
