About

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam (1931–2015) was an Indian aerospace scientist and 
            the 11th President of India. Known as the “Missile Man of India” for his 
            work in ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology, he inspired  
            millions throgh  his humility, vision, and dedication to education and 
            nation-building.

Timeline

  • 1931: Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu
  • 1958: Graduated from Madras Institute of Technology
  • 1969: Joined ISRO; led the SLV-III project
  • 1998: Key role in Pokhran-II nuclear tests
  • 2002-2007: Served as the 11th President of India
  • 2015: Passed away while delivering a lecture at IIM Shillong
